Strategic Career Guidance

30+ YEARS ACROSS FINANCE & CRE

Mike Strug brings over 30 years of experience across finance, commercial real estate, and institutional capital markets, helping students and young professionals navigate highly competitive industries with practical insight, strategic positioning, and long-term career direction.

Throughout his career, Mike has worked with global firms and advisory organizations including Vinadium Group, BlackRock, Nuveen, Prudential, Cushman & Wakefield, Assurant, and Capital Markets teams, developing expertise across investment analysis, recruiting, advisory, and relationship-driven business strategy.

His mentorship focuses on helping ambitious students gain clarity, confidence, and access to opportunities that are often difficult to navigate without experienced industry guidance.
